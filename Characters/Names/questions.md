# Naming Questions

*Questions about a character's name — what it carries, where it comes from, and how it functions in the story.*

---

## Origin & Meaning
- What is the character's full name?
- What cultural, ethnic, or linguistic tradition does the name come from?
- Does the name have a meaning — literal or symbolic — that reflects who they are?
- Was the name chosen with intention by their parents, or is it coincidental?
- Does the meaning of the name say something true about them, or something ironic?

---

## Usage & Relationship
- What do different people call them? (Full name, nickname, title, term of endearment, slur?)
- Do they go by a nickname? Where did it come from?
- Is there a name from their past they've left behind — a deadname, a married name, a childhood name?
- Did they ever choose or change their own name? Under what circumstances?
- Who is the only person allowed to call them by a specific name?

---

## Name as Story Device
- Does anyone use their name as a weapon — withholding it, mispronouncing it, weaponizing formality?
- Is there a moment where being called by the wrong name reveals something about the relationship?
- Is there a moment where being called by the right name — maybe for the first time — is significant?
- Does the name carry baggage from a family member who shared it?
- Is the name ordinary in their world, or does it mark them as different?
