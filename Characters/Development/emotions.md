Part 1: Genuine Emotions & Emotional States
These are direct experiences of feeling, whether fleeting or enduring.
A. Core Emotions & Their Intensities
Anger: Contemptuous, Detestation, Disdainful, Exasperation, Irascibility, Lividness, Resent(ful), Vengefulness
Sadness/Grief: Anticipatory Grief, Despondency, Dispiritedness, Dolor, Forsakenness, Glumness, Lugubriousness, Melancholia, Misery, Mournful, Sorrow, Woe
Fear/Anxiety: Angst, Apprehension, Dread (Existential), Foreboding, Misgiving, Panic, Trepidation
Joy/Happiness: Effervescence, Elation, Exhilaration, Glee, Jocundity, Jollity, Jubilance, Merriment, Mirth, Rapture
Love/Affection: Adoration, Infatuation, Longing, Lovesickness, Tenderness
Disgust: Distaste, Loathing, Repugnance, Revulsion
