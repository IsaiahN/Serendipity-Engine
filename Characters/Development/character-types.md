# Character Types

## Protagonists
Hero / Heroine
Antihero / Antiheroine
Tragic hero
Main protagonist
Villain protagonist
Supporting protagonist

## Antagonists
Villain (foiling protagonist — a direct personal opposition)
Villain (unrelated to protagonist — systemic evil harming the protagonist through cascading/downstream effects they may never directly see)
Antagonist who is not evil — simply injudicious, obstructive, or unlikeable
Antihero as antagonist
Non-personal antagonist (society, nature, time, illness, fate)
