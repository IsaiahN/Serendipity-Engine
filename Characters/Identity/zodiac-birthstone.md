## Elements

**Fire** — Aries, Leo, Sagittarius
Energetic, passionate, impulsive, courageous, self-driven. Burns bright; can consume.

**Earth** — Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn
Grounded, practical, patient, reliable, sensual. Builds slowly; deeply rooted.

**Air** — Gemini, Libra, Aquarius
Intellectual, communicative, social, curious, abstract. Moves freely; hard to pin down.

**Water** — Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces
Intuitive, emotional, empathetic, perceptive, deeply feeling. Runs deep; can flood or recede.

---

## Modalities

**Cardinal** — Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn
Initiators. Start things. Drive change. Can struggle to finish.

**Fixed** — Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius
Sustainers. Hold their ground. Determined and stubborn in equal measure.

**Mutable** — Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces
Adapters. Flexible, transitional, sometimes scattered. Bridge one season to the next.

---

## Date of Birth Mechanic
1. Assign or roll a zodiac sign
2. Pull the corresponding month(s) and element
3. Select a birthstone from that month
4. Pick a specific day within the date range
5. Use the resulting full date as the character's date of birth
