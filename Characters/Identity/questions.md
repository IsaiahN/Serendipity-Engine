# Identity Questions

*Questions about who a character is at the level of self — gender, faith, sexuality, and the social reality those create in their world.*

---

## Gender
- What is this character's gender identity?
- Was their gender ever questioned, policed, or erased by others?
- Do they present their gender differently in different contexts (home vs. work vs. strangers)?
- Has their understanding of their own gender shifted over time?
- Does the world of the story accept, tolerate, or persecute their gender identity?

---

## Sexuality & Romantic Orientation
- What is their sexual orientation? Their romantic orientation? (These can differ.)
- Are they out — to themselves, to others, to the world?
- Has their orientation caused them harm, joy, or both?
- Do they have a complicated relationship with any aspect of their own desire?
- Has anyone in their life made them feel ashamed of who they're attracted to?

---

## Faith & Belief
- What religion or spiritual tradition were they raised in?
- Do they still practice? Have they left it, modified it, or returned to it after leaving?
- If they left — what drove them out? A person, an event, a slow erosion?
- If they stayed — what does it give them that nothing else does?
- What do they believe about death, the afterlife, and what comes next?
- Does their faith create conflict with others in the story?

---

## Identity in the World
- Which parts of their identity are visible to strangers?
- Which parts do they conceal, and from whom?
- What assumptions do others make about them that are wrong?
- Has their identity ever been used against them?
- Has it ever saved them, or opened a door?
- Do they feel at home in their own identity, or still finding their way into it?
